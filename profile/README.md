## About ChistaDATA Inc.

**ChistaDATA Inc.** is the industry's leading full-stack **ClickHouse infrastructure operations** provider, delivering enterprise-grade consultative support and managed services to organizations that demand sub-second analytics at petabyte scale.

Headquartered in the **San Francisco Bay Area** with **11 global offices**, our engineering bench operates 24×7×365, staffed exclusively by senior ClickHouse engineers — not generalist consultants. We serve enterprises across AdTech, fintech, observability, telecom, SaaS, and Generative AI with a single engineering standard: **100% open-source ClickHouse, zero vendor lock-in**.

> *"ClickHouse is not just another column store. ClickHouse is the engine that makes real-time analytics economically possible at petabyte scale — and engineering it correctly is the difference between a dashboard that ships and a dashboard that scales."*
> > — **Shiv Iyer**, Founder & CEO, ChistaDATA Inc.
